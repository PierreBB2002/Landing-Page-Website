Description

This page will represent the most famous models of planes which belong to the famous plane manifacturing company called airbus, since this company has a long history with producing planes.

Airbus A320

With the largest single-aisle cabin in the sky, A320s provide passengers a roomy cabin from the time they board, and they can take advantage of the Airbus standard, which calls for 18-inch-wide economy seats. Operators can match the right aircraft size to route demand thanks to the true family concept and built-in versatility of the A320 product line. This covers the whole market, from low- to high-density routes, as well as longer-range transcontinental services that can be tailored to passenger capacities.

Airbus A330

The A330 Family, which flies all over the globe in activities ranging from passenger, freight, and VIP flights to military transport and aerial refueling, combines widebody luxury with constant innovation.

Airbus A350

The A350 offers the ultimate passenger experience even on the longest of flights. Its innovative design delivers a true feeling of comfort, with wide seats, high ceilings and alluring ambient lighting for all passanger comfort.

Airbus A380

Since going into service, the venerable A380 has completed over 800,000 flights with over 300 million passengers. The A380 will continue to fly for many years to come even if production is coming to an end, and Airbus is still providing complete support for A380 operators and their fleets.
